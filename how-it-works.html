The Process

Straightforward from start to close.

We've refined this process across years of transactions to be as efficient and transparent as possible for serious sellers.

I

Initial Contact

Reach out by email or through our contact form. Describe your holdings — type, quantity, form, and approximate purity if known. There is no commitment required at this stage, and all inquiries are held in confidence.

What to include: Type of gold (bars, coins, jewelry, scrap, etc.), approximate quantity or weight, condition, and your general location. The more detail you provide, the faster we can respond meaningfully.
II

Confidential Consultation

We'll follow up promptly — typically within one business day — to discuss the specifics of your holdings, answer your questions, and determine whether assay testing or additional documentation is needed before we can price the transaction.

What happens here: We'll walk through what you have, explain how we value it, and be upfront about any steps needed before a formal offer can be issued. No pressure, no obligation.
III

Formal Written Offer

You receive a formal written offer based on verified LBMA spot pricing and the specific characteristics of your holdings. The offer is clearly documented, time-stamped, and explained — you'll understand exactly how the number was reached.

Pricing basis: All offers reference the London Bullion Market Association (LBMA) spot price at the time of the offer, adjusted for purity, form, and condition. We share our calculation openly.
IV

Transaction & Settlement

Once you accept the offer, we coordinate logistics, physical transfer, and payment through a secure, fully documented process — structured to protect both parties throughout. Payment is made via secure wire transfer.

Payment & logistics: All terms including payment method, timing, and transfer logistics are agreed upon in writing before any gold changes hands. We handle the coordination — you focus on the outcome.
