What We Buy

Gold in virtually every form and origin, at the quantities that matter.

Our Scope

Any form. Any origin. Serious quantities.

We acquire gold across a wide range of forms and origins. If you're unsure whether your holdings qualify, reach out — we'll give you a straight answer with no obligation.

Bullion Bars

Investment-grade bars from recognized refiners and private mints, in any size.

  • LBMA Good Delivery bars (400 oz)
  • Kilobars (32.15 troy oz)
  • 100 oz, 10 oz, 1 oz bars
  • Fractional bars
  • Any recognized assayer or hallmark
  • Unassayed bars (subject to verification)

Bullion Coins

Government-minted and recognized bullion coins from all major issuing countries.

  • South African Krugerrands
  • American Gold Eagles & Buffalos
  • Canadian Maple Leafs
  • Austrian Philharmonics
  • British Sovereigns & Britannias
  • Australian Kangaroos & Nuggets
  • Chinese Gold Pandas

Numismatic & Pre-1933 Coins

Collectible and historic coins evaluated for both gold content and numismatic value.

  • U.S. pre-1933 gold coins
  • European historic gold coinage
  • Certified (PCGS / NGC) coins
  • Raw and uncertified collections
  • Partial and complete date sets

Gold Jewelry

Commercial and fine jewelry valued on gold content, evaluated fairly at current spot.

  • 10K, 14K, 18K, 22K, 24K pieces
  • Estate and antique jewelry
  • Broken or damaged pieces
  • Watches with gold cases or bands
  • Bulk jewelry lots from retailers

Scrap & Industrial Gold

Recovered and industrial-grade gold from a variety of commercial sources.

  • Dental gold (crowns, bridges, inlays)
  • Electronic scrap & circuit boards
  • Gold-plated reclaim
  • Sweeps and floor sweeps
  • Refinery residues

Raw & Mined Gold

Directly sourced gold from mining operations, in all forms from field to foundry.

  • Placer gold (nuggets & dust)
  • Hard rock mine production
  • Doré bars
  • Gold concentrates
  • Artisanal and small-scale mining output

Estate & Trust Holdings

Collections and portfolios requiring coordinated, documented liquidation.

  • Full estate gold collections
  • Trust-held bullion and coins
  • Safe deposit box contents
  • Combined coin and jewelry lots
  • Executors and administrators welcome

Dealer & Institutional Lots

Wholesale acquisition from dealers, importers, and companies liquidating gold assets.

  • Coin dealer inventory buyouts
  • Pawn shop gold portfolios
  • Jewelry manufacturer scrap
  • Corporate asset liquidations
  • Import/export consignments
