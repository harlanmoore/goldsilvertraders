Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to what
sellers ask us most.

What is the minimum quantity you purchase?
We focus on bulk transactions, but we evaluate each inquiry individually. As a general guideline, sellers with 10 or more troy ounces of gold will receive our full attention. For very large holdings — kilograms and above — we have dedicated processes in place.
How do you determine the price you offer?
Our offers are anchored to the LBMA spot price at the time of the transaction, adjusted for the form, purity, and condition of the gold. We are transparent about the calculation — you'll see exactly how the offer is derived.
Do I need to ship my gold before receiving an offer?
No. We provide preliminary offers based on your description and documentation before any gold changes hands. Physical verification, if required, is coordinated only after terms are agreed upon in principle.
Can you handle international transactions?
Yes. We have experience facilitating cross-border gold transactions in compliance with applicable export and import regulations. International sellers are welcome to reach out, and we'll discuss the specifics of your situation directly.
How is payment made?
Payment is made via secure wire transfer upon completion of the transaction. All terms, including payment method and timing, are agreed upon in writing before any transfer of gold takes place.
Is my inquiry kept confidential?
Absolutely. All inquiries, communications, and transaction details are held in strict confidence. We never share seller information with third parties without explicit consent.
What documentation will I need to provide?
Requirements vary by transaction size and origin of the gold. At minimum, standard identification and proof of ownership are required. We'll walk you through any documentation needs during our initial consultation — nothing will catch you off guard.
