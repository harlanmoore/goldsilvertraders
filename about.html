Career History

A path built on entrepreneurship.

Before gold, there were other businesses. Each one built the instincts that make for a trustworthy, decisive buyer today.

Early 2000s

Falcon Trading — Electronics

Founded and grew an electronics company during the dot-com era. Built it into a going concern significant enough to attract acquisition interest, then sold it to Ableauctions.com at the height of the boom.

Mid 2000s

Real Estate & Foreclosures

Pivoted into real estate, buying and selling foreclosure properties and launching a multiple listing service specifically for foreclosures — an early example of using technology to create market visibility in an opaque asset class.

Late 2000s

A Trip That Changed Everything

A nonprofit trip to Africa was supposed to be a short detour. Instead, it became a turning point. The continent — its energy, its people, its raw commerce — got under his skin. He sold the real estate business and didn't look back.

2000s – 2010s

Gold Buyer — Democratic Republic of Congo

Moved to the DRC and spent years as an on-the-ground gold buyer in one of the world's most active alluvial gold-producing regions. Developed deep knowledge of sourcing, valuation, logistics, and the realities of operating in frontier markets.

2010s

Gold Buyer — Solomon Islands

Relocated to the Solomon Islands in the South Pacific, continuing gold buying operations in another significant alluvial region. Built relationships with local producers and government stakeholders across several years of active operation.

Ongoing

Gold & Silver Traders — U.S. & International

Operated twelve gold buying locations across the Seattle metropolitan area while maintaining international gold trade operations. Today, Gold & Silver Traders serves sellers across the United States and internationally — bringing the same direct, knowledgeable approach to every transaction.
